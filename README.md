## Sustainability Impact

By choosing to walk or bike instead of driving:

- 🌍 **Carbon Emissions:** A typical car emits ~0.2 kg CO₂ per km. Over a 4 km commute, that's **~0.8 kg CO₂ saved per trip**, or **~200 kg per year** (assuming 250 work days).
- 💰 **Cost Savings:** Walking and biking cost virtually nothing compared to gas (~$0.50/day), parking (~$10–15/day in downtown Boulder), and vehicle maintenance.
- 💪 **Health Benefits:** Regular walking or biking commutes improve cardiovascular health, reduce stress, boost mood and productivity, and build endurance.
- 🚗 **Traffic Reduction:** Fewer cars on the road means less congestion for everyone in Boulder.

## Tips for Sustainable Commuting in Boulder

1. **Use Boulder's Bike Lanes:** The city has an extensive network of bike lanes, including Paths along Canyon Boulevard and 19th Street.
2. **Check the Weather:** Colorado weather can change quickly — dress in layers and keep rain gear handy.
3. **Combine Trips:** Make the most of each trip by running errands on your way to or from work.
4. **Use a Bike Rack:** Secure your bike at designated racks near Broadway and downtown Boulder.
5. **Walk with a Buddy:** The Pearl Street Mall area is a great place to walk — enjoy the scenery and urban amenities!
6. **Stay Safe:** Use lights and reflective gear when commuting in low-light conditions.
7. **Plan Your Route:** Boulder's flat terrain in the city center makes for easy, enjoyable walking and biking.
